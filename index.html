About Me
I am an undergraduate student at Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, Georgia, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am also passionate about art and design, and hope to use my creativity to improve how humans and machines interact physically and intellectually.

Entrepreneurship and collaboration thrills me and I aspire to use advanced technology to empower mankind. I highly value community service and hope that more of the under-served community can reap the benefits of technology's exponential growth.

Feel free to contact me to share your experiences and advice or just to get to know me!
